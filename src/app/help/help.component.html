Integrations

If you have an online store on an e-commerce platform or you have your own developed website, follow these steps:
  1. Enter Account and then Integrations to create the data to integrate yourself in a single click.
  2. Paste the data into the e-commerce platform or use our public API.
  3. Done! You are already starting to offer OrdersNow Shipments as a delivery method.
